About LeafLine

LeafLine is an AI-powered web application for plant disease detection, created as the final project for our AI course.

The app allows users to upload a plant leaf image and instantly receive a prediction of the plant type and its health status using a deep learning model. Our goal is to make plant disease diagnosis more accessible and efficient for everyone.
